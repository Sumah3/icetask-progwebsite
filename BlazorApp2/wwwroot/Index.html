DETROIT :

BECOME HUMAN

OVERVIEW :

2018 | Action Adventure | Mature

Detroit: Become Human is an adventure game developed by Quantic Dream and released in 2018. The game is set in a futuristic version of Detroit, where androids have become an integral part of society, performing various tasks for humans.

The story follows three main android characters:

Connor: A prototype android assigned to the Detroit Police Department to hunt down deviant androids.

Kara: A domestic android who becomes deviant to protect a young girl from her abusive father.

Markus: An in-home care android who becomes the leader of the android liberation movement.

The game is heavily narrative-driven, with player choices significantly impacting the story’s direction and outcome. It explores themes of artificial intelligence, consciousness, and what it means to be human
